The 27 Club is the unofficial name given to group of artists who

died tragically at the young age of 27. They were some of the

most talented creatives of their generation, and in their short

lives each made an enormous impact. Sadly, many

led hard-partying lifestyles, abusing drugs and alcohol. These are the musiciansand artists who make up the
